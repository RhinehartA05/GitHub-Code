The PB & J of Champions

Some assembly required...

  1. Grasp two slices of bread, a spoon, your favorite jelly, and some peanut-butter.

  2. Open the container of jelly, use the concave side of the spoon to scoop up the jelly; spread jelly on a slice of bread.

  3. Wash the spoon, open the container of peanut-butter. Use the convex side of the spoon to scrape up peanut-butter; spread PB on the other slice of bread.

  4. Combine both slices of bread so that the PB & J touch one another to make a sandwich

  5. Clean up after yourself, your mama raised you better.
